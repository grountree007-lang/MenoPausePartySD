What is a Pause Party?

A fun, safe space where women can ask questions, learn what’s happening in their bodies, share stories, and actually celebrate this new season of life.

MP
Founder-led & people-first Built to normalize menopause, not hide it.

Why menopause parties?

Most women enter perimenopause and menopause feeling alone, under-informed, and unsure where to turn. Pause Parties was created to change that — by turning a whispered topic into an honest, supportive, and even joyful experience.

We blend evidence-informed education with real-life conversation, movement, and ritual. The goal: help women feel seen, supported, and confident advocating for their health.

Education without overwhelm Community & connection Body & mind wellness Stigma-free conversation
What happens at a Pause Party?
  • Gentle intro to perimenopause & menopause basics
  • Guided conversation about common symptoms & experiences
  • Simple tools for sleep, mood, stress & hot flashes
  • Light movement or breathwork (optional)
  • Time to share stories, laugh, vent & feel heard

Every gathering is tailored to the group: intimate living-room circles, milestone “menopause celebrations,” or professional workplace sessions.

Who are Pause Parties for?

Anyone experiencing or approaching perimenopause or menopause, plus the people who love them. Parties can be all-women, mixed-gender, or tailored to your community.

Do you provide medical advice?

No. We offer education and general information, not diagnosis or treatment. Guests are encouraged to discuss personal medical questions with their own healthcare providers.
